About the Bank

Union Bank of India (UBI) is one of India's leading public sector banks. It was established on 11 November 1919 and has its headquarters in Mumbai, Maharashtra. The bank is owned primarily by the Government of India, which holds a majority stake. On 1 April 2020, Andhra Bank and Corporation Bank were merged with Union Bank of India, making it one of the largest public sector banks in the country. Key Information
